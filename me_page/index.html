Hot Takes on Hot Food

Happy to debate any of the following. Some of these are probably not that hot if you are Chinese.

  • Dim Sum places often serve items in threes. Choosing a prime number of food items to be split among multiple diners causes unnecessary drama.
  • Dim Sum places should serve items in 12s (thus evenly splittable among 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, or 12 diners).
  • That said, 3-piece Dim Sum incentivizes ordering more Dim Sum to get everyone a fair share, so maybe they're just being business-savvy.
  • The Dim Sum tick-box order sheet is how all restaurants should do ordering.
  • 小笼包 should be eaten in one glorious, mouth-burning bite.
  • Asian and Middle Eastern cuisines are irritatingly underpriced compared to the likes of Italian and French cuisine.
